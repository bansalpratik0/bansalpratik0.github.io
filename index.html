About Me

 

Hey! I am Pratik Bansal, a senior at Purdue University, studying in the department of Electrical and Computer Engineering. I am currently pursuing a bachelors' degree in Electrical Engineering. My main focuses are Automation and Autonomous Systems. I am fascinated by cars and I believe in a quote by Enzo Ferrari, "WHAT'S BEHIND YOU DOESN'T MATTER"

A little about where I am from:

I was born in West Bengal, India and I have spent 16 years of my childhood in Lagos, Nigeria. When my friends ask me where I am from, I like to say, "I am an Indian, who has lived in Nigeria all my life and I'm now studying in the States." It takes a while to digest that!
